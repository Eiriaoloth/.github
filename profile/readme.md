Welcome to Avenoria, a lush and beautiful land scarred by fire-scorched earth and cut in twain by deserts and rivers. Mountains loom on the map, casting shadows of a marvelous length. Avenoria is, on short, a land of dragons. The Destroyer is coming, ready to clasp Avenoria in his jaws. Who can stop him now? Certainly not the villagers of Fornitas, or even the more powerful Guardians. The doom marches on, unchecked. But the Redeemer is there, and Avenoria shall not fall while He still breathes. 

The question is, how long will that be?

Eiriaoloth is a narrow valley surrounded by a loop of the mountains, with the East side visible to the sun. The Pillars are tall, rune-scarred posts sticking straight up out of the land.
Welcome to the valley of Eiriaoloth.
